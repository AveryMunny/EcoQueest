EcoQuest Controls 🌱

Movement: W A S D or Arrow Keys

Collect Resource: Space

Plant Tree: P (Cost: 1 wood)

Tips:

  • Trees 🌳 give wood but reduce ecosystem health.
  • Berries 🍓 give food and improve the ecosystem.
  • Coal ⛏️ gives energy but heavily damages the environment.
  • Renewables ☀️🌀 give passive clean energy each turn.
