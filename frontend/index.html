EcoQuest Controls 🌱

Movement

W A S D or Arrow Keys — Move

Space — Collect resources

E — Exit house

Building

P — Plant Tree (Cost: 1 Wood)

1 — Build Solar Panel (Cost: 2 Wood)

2 — Build Wind Turbine (Cost: 3 Wood)

3 — Build House (Cost: 5 Wood)

4 — Build Farm Plot (Cost: 2 Wood)

Farming

5 — Plant Wheat Seed

6 — Plant Carrot Seed

X — Harvest Crops

Wildlife

🐇 🦌 🐦 appear when ecosystem health ≥ 70

Tips

  • Trees 🌳 give wood but lower ecosystem health.
  • Berries 🍓 restore food and improve ecosystem health.
  • Coal ⛏️ gives energy but harms the environment.
  • Renewables ☀️ 🌀 provide clean passive energy.
  • Crops grow over real-time minutes.
